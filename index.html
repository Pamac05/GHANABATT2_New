Welcome to GHANBATT Abyei

The Ghanaian Battalion (GHANBATT) serves as part of the United Nations Interim Security Force for Abyei (UNISFA), demonstrating Ghana's commitment to international peace and security. Our mission is to protect civilians, facilitate humanitarian assistance, and support the implementation of agreements between Sudan and South Sudan.

Since deployment, GHANBATT has been instrumental in maintaining peace and stability in the Abyei region, earning recognition for professionalism, discipline, and dedication to the peacekeeping mandate.

Mission Highlights

Protection of Civilians

Ensuring the safety and security of local communities in the Abyei region

Peacekeeping Operations

Maintaining ceasefire and preventing armed conflict between parties

Humanitarian Support

Facilitating delivery of humanitarian aid and essential services

Monitoring

Observing and reporting on security situation and violations
